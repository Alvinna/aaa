# Cultural Groups 
## Ellakians
### Appearance
**Humanoid** with **distinct** feature often adapted to living environment, but may also
be purely aesthetic. Ex, grayish skin to blend into rocky environment
or horns and tail for aesthetic.

Due to their ability to shape-shift, **aesthetics** play important roles in
Ellakians culture. One's clothing style is one major way of self-expression. Ex,
cape with emblem, detailed floral dresses made from rare silk.

### Crafts
Architectures and crafts differ from region to region due to species' robust
ability to adapt. However, they generally appear more **Bio-inspired**, with
**intricate carving and decor**. Designs that appear **almost like an
art-piece**, yet hide **powerful** technology underneath is highly praised. Ex,
personal speed-bike made of smooth, wood-like material with warm lighting,
equipped with state-of-the-art silent thrusters.

### Food
Due to the highly diverse culture, Ellakians cuisines are major source of
gourmet delights. 

## Human 
### Appearance
They're human.
No, but really. Humans have, perhaps prematurely, taken to body modification as a
medium for fashion, utility, and security. Attitudes vary on the subject depending
on planet and faction, and modifications can range from cybernetic lenses to
full replacement of limbs with kinetic weaponry. Culturally, many stricter traditions
were lost to expansion and interplay with other species. As a result, human stylistic
and cultural choices are heavily dependent on their planet and faction, e.g. heavy use
of body modification on NovaLife planets, Wayfarers take influence from a variety of
Earth cultures, etc.

## Istrians
### Appearance
Istrians signal their mood through their colors and their stance - when they
are relaxed, they take on a jellyfish-like pose. Under threat, they spread their
limbs fully, appearing like a star. Their movement is generally very controlled,
appearing to crawl with their many legs, but in combat, they tend to revert to
their more natural motion that is more like a "whirl". Their colors are difficult
but not impossible for other beings to parse, and automatons are the best at doing so.

### Crafts
Istrians and rely on color to communicate, so many of their stylistic choices in
buildings and spacecraft are fairly dull color-wise, with brownish colors ranging
from brick to bone. Their ships and technology are physically flexible, a mindset
that stems from their own shape. One popular ship chassis is a vast net wherein
the "strands" are occupied, and different nodes of the net contain a number of
thrusters.

### Food
Istrians's hardiness extends to their digestive systems and they can consume just about
anything in the galaxy - however, most of their preferred delicacies and local staples
are heavily toxic to every other known species.

## Automaton
### Appearance
Automatons are allowed a singular form in their lifetimes, and modification to these is
generally minimal. They have a great deal of respect for human discomfort, and avoid
forms that look too close to human, though they still may choose android bodies.

### Crafts
Their ancestral home is extremely geometric in nature, fitting the built-in aesthetic senses of most automatons.
Their architecture and style in other places fits the same mold, generally using metallic colors.
